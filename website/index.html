What it is

Three layers. One surface.

gKestral is not another chatbot. It is a desktop workbench that gathers, reasons, produces, and executes -- all from one window.

01

Information tool

Gather research, emails, files, and live web sources into a single context window. Stop copy-pasting between tabs.

02

Document engine

Turn scattered inputs into briefs, reports, presentations, and plans. Real DOCX and PPTX files, not markdown previews.

03

Execution layer

Code, automation, and file operations run behind the scenes when needed. The user directs. The machine executes.

Who it serves

For people who finish things.

Same tool. Same loop. Whether you're writing a thesis or closing a deal.

Research

Students and researchers

Source PDFs, synthesize findings across hundreds of pages, produce documents with accurate citations. Your workspace persists between sessions.

Operations

Founders and operators

Email to action. Market intelligence to decision brief. Incoming chaos to outgoing clarity, with documents saved to your folders.

Technical

Technical builders

Screenshot to fix. Codebase analysis. Architecture reasoning. The full power of a coding agent, wrapped in a workspace that non-engineers can also use.

Why Gemini

The model is the product.

gKestral is Gemini-native. Every design decision exploits what Gemini does better than anything else.

1M token context

Hold your entire project, history, and documentation in memory. No chunking, no retrieval hacks.

Search grounding

Live research with cited sources from Google Search. Not stale training data. Real-time reality.

Multimodal input

Screenshots, PDFs, diagrams, images as first-class context. Not attachments. Working material.

Context caching

90% cost reduction on repeated queries. Your workspace stays warm. Economics that scale.

What makes it different

Clear about what it is.
Clearer about what it isn't.

Not another coding CLI

gKestral is an information tool. Code runs behind the scenes to produce documents, process data, and manage files. The user never needs to see it.

Not a chatbot

It produces artifacts. Briefs, presentations, research, images, code. The conversation is the input. The output is the work.

Not browser-bound

Sessions persist locally. Your workspace is folders on your computer. No browser tab loss. No cloud-only lock-in.

Not model-agnostic

Gemini-native by design. Every feature -- caching, grounding, multimodal, million-token context -- is built to exploit what Gemini does best.
