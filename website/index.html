Story

Desktop work is still fragmented. The models are ready. The product layer is not.

People already use AI for research, planning, writing, visuals, and coding. What they still do not have is one desktop tool that makes those flows feel coherent. gKestral is built to be that missing workbench.

It packages Gemini into a product that feels practical every day: less tab switching, less copy-paste, more grounded output, and a stronger path from information to action.

What Changes

  • One surface replaces the usual spread of tabs and tools.
  • Documents become outputs, not separate chores.
  • Visuals, files, and context live inside the same loop.

Product

Product Thesis

A Gemini-native desktop layer for managing information and producing finished work.

gKestral is designed to feel useful before it feels impressive. It helps users gather what matters, understand it faster, and convert it into output from the same place.
